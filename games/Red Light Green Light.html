About the Game

Red Light Green Light is a timeless children's game that challenges players to move forward when the light is green and freeze when it turns red. This digital version brings the classic playground game to your screen with modern graphics and smooth gameplay.

Perfect for players of all ages, this game tests your timing, patience, and quick reflexes. Whether you're playing solo or competing with friends, Red Light Green Light offers endless entertainment and excitement.

How to Play

  • Press any key or click to start moving when the light is green
  • Freeze immediately when the light turns red
  • If you're caught moving during a red light, you'll be sent back to the start
  • Be the first to reach the finish line to win!
